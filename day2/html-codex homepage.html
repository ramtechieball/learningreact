Coraluxe - Hotel & Resort Website Template

Coraluxe is a premium-quality hotel & resort website template designed for hotels, beach resorts, villas, guest houses, and luxury island accommodations. Whether you’re running a tropical beach resort, a boutique hotel, or a holiday rental business, Coraluxe provides a clean and elegant layout that highlights your property with style. Its visually appealing design, combined with well-structured sections, makes it an excellent choice for showcasing rooms, amenities, gallery, and guest experiences in a professional way.

Built with a fully responsive and mobile-first approach, Coraluxe ensures your website looks flawless on all devices, from desktops to smartphones. This beach resort website template is optimized for fast loading performance, smooth navigation, and user-friendly interaction. Carefully crafted layouts and optimized assets help improve page speed and user engagement, providing a seamless browsing experience for your visitors and potential guests.

The template comes in both free and pro versions to suit different needs. The free version includes a beautifully designed homepage, ideal for basic use or testing. The pro version expands functionality with multiple inner pages such as Rooms, Room Details, Blog, FAQs, Gallery, Services, Staff, Offers, Reservation, Testimonials, 404, and Contact page, including a fully functional PHP and AJAX contact form. This makes Coraluxe a complete resort Bootstrap template solution for building a professional and feature-rich hotel or resort website.
